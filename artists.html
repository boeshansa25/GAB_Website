49 Winchester

49 Winchester, hailing from Russell County, Virginia, storms onto the national stage with their latest album "Fortune Favors The Bold," a fusion of tear-in-your-beer alt-country, gritty barroom rock-n-roll, and electrifying Appalachian folk. Led by lead singer/guitarist Isaac Gibson, the band's evolution from its humble beginnings in Castlewood, Virginia, reflects a journey of self-discovery and musical exploration. "Fortune Favors The Bold" isn't just an album; it's a testament to resilience, a defiance against odds, and a celebration of the hard-earned spirit that defines their journey. As they navigate through the landscapes of their sound, 49 Winchester stands as a beacon of authenticity and raw passion, embodying the essence of chasing dreams with unwavering determination.

Futurebirds

Futurebirds is a rock band hailing from Athens, Georgia, known for their eclectic blend of indie, country, and psych-rock influences. With their latest EP "Bloomin’ Too," the band celebrates 13 years together, showcasing their unique musical style and iron will. Led by singer/guitarists Carter King and Daniel Womack, Futurebirds' collaborative spirit and blue-collar work ethic shine through in their seamless partnership with producer Carl Broemel and their dynamic live performances, emphasizing their deep connection with their fan base.

Boy Named Banjo

Boy Named Banjo, born from the streets of Nashville where a chance encounter led to their moniker, brings a vibrant blend of bluegrass harmonies, distorted guitars, and rock and roll drums in their electrifying new album, "Dusk." Led by Barton Davies and William Reames, the band's evolution from a high school trio to a Nashville quintet is evident in their diverse sound and keen songwriting. With influences ranging from Music Row to Laurel Canyon, Boy Named Banjo's major label debut on Mercury Records captures the essence of their dynamic live performances, blending party anthems with introspective meditations on love, loss, and growth. As they navigate the complexities of youth and heartbreak, "Dusk" serves as a captivating journey, promising more chapters to come in their evolving story.
